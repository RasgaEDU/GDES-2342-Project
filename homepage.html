My Office

Why Build One?

Having a strong workspace is a great way to boost creativity and workflow. Especially when working from home and doing school from home. It is imperative to have a good workspace for productivity.

Goals in Mind when Making

I knew I wanted something that focuses on these main goals. It had to have all the technology I needed. It needed to be roomy. It needed to be ergonomic to have healthy work from home, and some good decorations to help keep myself visually stimulated while I work 9-14 hours a day in my office.
